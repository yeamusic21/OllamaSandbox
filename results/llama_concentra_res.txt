What is Streptococcus Pyogenes?

Streptococcus pyogenes, or Group A streptococcus (GAS), is a facultative Gram-positive coccus which grows in chains and causes numerous infections in humans including pharyngitis, tonsillitis, scarlet fever, cellulitis, erysipelas, rheumatic fever, post-streptococcal glomerulonephritis and necrotizing fasciitis.
